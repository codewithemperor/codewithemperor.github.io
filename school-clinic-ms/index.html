    School Clinic Management System

    The School Clinic Management System is a full-stack web development project implemented using PHP, offering a comprehensive solution for healthcare administration within educational institutions. With its intuitive user interface, clinic staff can efficiently manage patient appointments, medical records, and inventory. This PHP-based system ensures seamless integration across the front-end and back-end components, facilitating smooth clinic operations and effective healthcare delivery to students and staff.
